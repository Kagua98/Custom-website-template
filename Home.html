New Item Sale
  • Click on Make sale.
  • Note: For saved items just select saved item pick the relevant item to be sold and edit.
  • Insert the required fields: Buyer PIN (Option for those claiming the VAT), Product Name, Quantity, Unit Price, Unit of Measure, Discount (Optional), Tax rate (16% automatic), HS Code (0%, 8%, and exempt)
  • Click on Submit Sale
  • Select the payment methods
  • Enter the amount paid
  • Submit sale
  • Then click on print and submit

Generate Credit Note
  • –
    Click on Credit
  • –
    Input the CU Invoice number e.g. 881007528000000275
  • –
    Note: Just add the last digits after the zeros instead of the whole number e.g. in this case only input 275
  • –
    Click Next
  • –
    Print and Submit the credit note
Debit Note
  • –
    Click on Debit
  • –
    Input the CU Invoice number e.g 8810075280000000275
  • –
    Note: Just like in Credit Note, only input the last 3 digits e.g. in this case 275
  • –
    Click add item or edit the item on display
  • –
    Click Next
  • –
    Print and Submit the debit Note
